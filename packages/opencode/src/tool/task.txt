Track persistent work items. Tasks have IDs like `T1` or `T1.2` and move through `open`, `in_progress`, `blocked`, `done`, or `abandoned`.

Wrap arguments in `operation`.

Actions:
- `create`, `list`, `get`
- `start`, `block`, `unblock`
- `done`, `abandon`, `rename`

Use this for multi-step work that needs tracking across turns or for subagents. Mark a task `done` only when the work is actually complete; otherwise keep it in progress or blocked.

Examples:
{"operation":{"action":"create","summary":"Implement auth"}}
{"operation":{"action":"start","id":"T1"}}
{"operation":{"action":"done","id":"T1","event_summary":"all tests pass"}}
